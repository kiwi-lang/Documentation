◆ bAllowOutofRangeTargets

bool FGKAbilityTarget::bAllowOutofRangeTargets

If true, the target can be selected while out of range the players will start moving toward the enemy to reach the minimal range, if false the target will not be able to be selected and the user will have to get closer to the target to cast the spell

For actor targeting, allowing out of range target selection gives a better experience as they do not have to worry about the ability range as much

For point targeting, allowing out of range target selection could give WORSE experience depending on the ability. Sometimes you want to execute the ability in a generic direction which in that case it could help Other times you want the ability to cast at a very specific location
